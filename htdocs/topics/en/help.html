Selecting Words

The following options are awailable for selecting words:

  • A part of speach
  • Subject domains

A part of speach and a subject domain give an intersection. For instance, if Verbs and Cooking are selected at the same time, then you get a list verbs that relate to cooking.

A few subject domains give a union. Say, if Cooking and Holidays are selected at the same time, then you get a list words related to cooking and words related to holidays as well. Use this vocabulary to discuss an upcoming party with your mates.

To select a part of speach:

  • Select a part of speach in the Part of speach drop-down list

To deselect a part of speach:

  • Select the item All in the Part of speach drop-down list

To select a subject domain:

  • Click on a deselected subject domain tag

To deselect a subject domain:

  • Click on a selected subject domain tag
